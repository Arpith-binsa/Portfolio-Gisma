Why I shoot Sony. And will never Change

June 15, 2025 · 2 min read


The RAW file after taking the group Photo for Blau-Weiss


I started off my Photography journey in 2016, shooting on a Canon Ixus 185 digital camera. A camera that started my passion for photography and also one that fueled my love for canon for the coming years.

This was the camera I begged my mom to get me to start taking pictures. And was supposed to be the camera with which I was supposed to start my YouTube Vlogging career with.

The image quality and the zoom capabilities of this camera amazed me, especially for a cheap and light Digital camera.

This love for canon carried on through the next few years where I upgraded from my Crusty old Ixus 185 to a canon EOS 4000d. A DSLR camera which at that time made me a star with my friend group. I later on also went onto upgrade to a Canon 200d M2 which was the biggest jump in quality and price I had made till then.

Even after being a Fanboy for canon when push came to shove and I was Planning on leaving my home country for Germany to pursue my future career, the dream camera I had in mind was the Sony a7M3. A titan of a camera, capable of both exception Video and Photo Quality.
